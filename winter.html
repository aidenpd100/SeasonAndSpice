Spiced Pumpkin Seeds

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ tablespoons margarine, melted
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic salt
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups raw whole pumpkin seeds

    • Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 275˚F
  • Combine the margarine, salt, garlic salt, Worcestershire sauce and pumpkin seeds. Mix thoroughly and place in shallow baking dish.
  • Bake for 1 hour, stirring occasionally
    • Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

    Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons mirin (sweet rice wine)
  • 2 tablespoons chili garlic sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup reduced-sodium chicken broth, divided
  • 2 cups instant brown rice
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 2 green onions, sliced

    • Instructions:

  • In a small bowl, mix the first six ingredients and 1/4 cup chicken broth until smooth. Cook rice according to package directions.
  • Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add broccoli; stir-fry 2 minutes. Add remaining broth; cook 1-2 minutes or until broccoli is crisp-tender. Stir sauce mixture and add to pan. Bring to a boil; cook and stir 1-2 minutes or until sauce is thickened.
  • Stir in chicken and green onions; heat through. Serve with rice.
    • White Hot Chocolate

    Ingredients:

  • 2 c. milk
  • 1 1/2 c. half-and-half
  • 3/4 c. white chocolate chips, plus more for topping
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • Mini marshmallows, for topping

    • Instructions:

  • Heat the milk and half-and-half in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir in ¾ cup white chocolate chips until melted, then stir in the salt and vanilla.
  • Pour into mugs and top with mini marshmallows and more white chocolate chips.
